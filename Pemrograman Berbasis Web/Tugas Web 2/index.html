肉麵JOURNEY

This is my Bakmi culinary journal. Bakmi means 'meat noodles' and is a flavorful noodle dish that originated from the cultural assimilation of Chinese immigrants with Indonesian culture.

This journal is filled with my stories of visiting Bakmi shops in Indonesia. I have tried many kinds of Bakmi styles, but my absolute favorite is Chicken Bakmi.
